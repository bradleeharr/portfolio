Functional Computer with Digital Logic Gates Project

This project involved creating a functional computer using digital logic gates and integrated circuit components. The design was based on the insightful tutorial by Ben Eater, providing a hands-on experience in understanding the fundamental principles of computer architecture and operation.

Design

The computer's design was a breadboard-based architecture, comprising several modules including the Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU), clock module, memory unit, and control logic. Digital logic gates and integrated circuit components were meticulously arranged to perform desired functions.

Implementation

Once the design was established, the next phase involved assembling the components on the breadboard and connecting them according to the design schematics. Great attention was paid to details such as ensuring the right connections and avoiding short circuits.

Testing and Results

The final stage was the testing phase, which involved verifying the functionality of each module and the system as a whole. The project was a success, with the computer being capable of executing simple instructions, thus showcasing the beauty and complexity of computer architecture.
