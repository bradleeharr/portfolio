Development of a Chess AI Project

The main goal of this project was to create a Chess AI that not only effectively plays the game but also personalizes its strategies and moves based on historical games played on Lichess. It employs machine learning to mimic a human player's style, thus enhancing the gameplay experience.

Machine Learning Model

To achieve this, a machine learning model was trained on a vast dataset of historical games from Lichess. The model used features like the position of the pieces on the board, past moves, and potential future moves. This training enabled the AI to recognize patterns and trends in human players' strategies and mimic them in its gameplay.

AI Integration

The trained machine learning model was then integrated into the Chess AI. With each move it makes, the AI analyzes the current state of the game and the potential outcomes of various moves, predicting the most likely successful move based on the machine learning model's insights.

Performance Evaluation

The final step involved rigorous testing and evaluation of the Chess AI. The AI played against various styles and levels of players, successfully emulating human playing styles and displaying a high level of strategic gameplay.
